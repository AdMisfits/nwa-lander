Frequently asked questions

How do I know if I need water treatment?

Common signs: white scale buildup on faucets, dry or itchy skin after bathing, spots on dishes, water that tastes or smells off, or stiff laundry. But many contaminants — lead, bacteria, nitrates — are invisible. A free water test is the only way to know what's actually in your water.

What does your free water test include?

We test for 100+ contaminants including hardness, chlorine, lead, iron, pH, TDS, bacteria, and nitrates. You get a full written report within 48 hours with clear explanations and comparisons to EPA guidelines.

How much does a water treatment system cost?

It depends on your water. Under-sink RO systems typically run $500–$1,200 installed. Whole-home softeners range $1,500–$3,500. We provide options at different price points and financing is available. No surprises — you know the cost before we start.

How long does installation take?

Most residential systems are installed in 2–4 hours. Many customers have clean water the same day. Our technicians handle everything: installation, testing, and a full walkthrough of your new system.

What cities does NWA of {city} serve?

We serve {city} and the entire surrounding metro area including Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Leander, and more. Same-day and next-day appointments usually available.

What's your satisfaction guarantee?

If you're not satisfied with your water quality after treatment, we make it right. We'll re-test, adjust, or replace components at no additional charge. That's backed by NWA's national guarantee.

Does NWA provide treatment for both city water and well water?

Yes. City water and well water have different challenges, and we have solutions for both. City water often needs chlorine removal and softening. Well water may need iron filtration, UV treatment, or more. We test first, then recommend the right approach.
